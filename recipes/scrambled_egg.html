Soft Boiled Egg

Description

This can be served as a side dish or an appitizer.

For this recipe, you will need:

Ingredients:

  • Egg(As much as you need)
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter
  • Parsley

Steps:

  1. Heat you pan.
  2. Add oil./li>
  3. When the oil starts smoking, add the egg.
  4. Add salt and butter and mix them
  5. Remove from the pan.
  6. Garnish with pepper and parsley.
