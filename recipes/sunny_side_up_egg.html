Sunny Side Up Egg

Description

This is everyone's side dish. A rich, creamy egg that goes with everything.

For this recipe, you will need:

Ingredients:

  • Egg(As much as you need)
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper(optional)

Steps:

  1. Heat up your pan.
  2. Add oil.
  3. When the oil starts to smoke, add the egg.
  4. Baste the egg.
  5. Add a pinch of salt.
  6. When the edges starts to brown, turn the heat off.
  7. Garnish it with salt and pepper./
