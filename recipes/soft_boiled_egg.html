Soft Boiled Egg

Description

This is an amazing addition to ramen and instant noodles.

For this recipe, you will need:

Ingredients:

  • Egg(As much as you need)
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Add about three cups to a pot.
  2. Add salt
  3. Heat it until it is boiling.
  4. Add the egg.
  5. Boil about 7 minutes
  6. Put the eggs in an ice bath.
  7. Remove the shells and we have a lovely creamy egg./
